Gelli plate first experiments
Just got my first gelli plate. it took a lot of trial and error to get a clear image. I found that my smo were too thin and dried too fast, but my rollerball ink had some success. I might buy a heavy body acrylic and see how that works. So far, the best process I have found is ~15 seconds of smoothing the magazine page onto the plate with light pressure, and then letting the plate sit on the final paper for at least a couple minutes. Pulling it off too soon means it will not dry and will not stick to the paper.
